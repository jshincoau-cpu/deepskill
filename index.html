What we do

We provide entry-level labour hire for regional operators who need reliable people on site, whether that’s indoors on the line or outdoors in the field.

  • General labour and production line workers
  • Packing, sorting and basic warehouse roles
  • Hygiene / sanitation and cleaning crews
  • Site-based and field support roles, including aquaculture support work

Training & Onboarding

We provide practical, entry-level training to prepare workers for both indoor and outdoor site operations. Our onboarding focuses on safety, productivity and clear communication so workers can start confidently from day one.

  • Basic site safety and PPE guidance
  • Production line and manual handling introduction
  • Hygiene, sanitation and workplace cleanliness standards
  • Cold storage and warehouse basics
  • Field operations support training for aquaculture and outdoor work

As we expand, we will also provide specialised training pathways for marine, aquaculture and diving-related operations.

About

Deepskill Workforce & Training is locally operated from Cressy, Tasmania, with management experience in high-volume operations, safety and onboarding. We also offer training and development programs to help workers build skills and advance their careers.

We focus on simple, clear communication and reliable placements rather than over-complicated systems.
